About Us

Tjantik Kebaya Collection has been a leader in traditional fashion for over 20 years. Founded on the principles of elegance, craftsmanship, and heritage, we specialize in creating beautiful, hand-tailored Kebayas that reflect the rich culture of Indonesia. Our vision is to preserve traditional artistry while introducing modern elegance.

Mission: To deliver exceptional, handcrafted Kebayas that blend heritage with modern luxury.
Vision: To be the leading brand in traditional Indonesian attire, known for innovation and quality.
Location: Jakarta, Indonesia.
